# **Main pattern from this batch**

The big correction for 2022-23 and 2023-24 is:

Bad-team usage and starting lineup necessity should not automatically create Heavy-Minute Starter status.

Examples:

Bojan Bogdanović on Detroit

Tyus Jones on Washington

Keldon Johnson after San Antonio shifted him

Jordan Clarkson / Sexton-type Utah scoring roles

Cam Johnson in Brooklyn

Tobias Harris as a low-burden veteran starter

The opposite issue also matters:

Low-usage elite starters should not be undergraded as Tactical Starter.

Examples:

Jrue Holiday

Derrick White

Herb Jones

Jaden McDaniels

Rudy Gobert

Brook Lopez

Mike Conley

That’s the right shrinkage logic: **usage-heavy players can shrink down, and low-usage trust players can move up.**
